Image Format Experiment

What is the most optimal format for this type of image and why? I chose PNG for its lossless quality, suitability for complex images, and broad compatibility on the web.

What happens when we save it in JPEG format? Saving in JPEG results in a loss of transparency and quality and lossy compression. While it reduces file size and is suitable for photographs, it may compromise detailed or transparent images.

If our website has a white background, what format would be the most optimal and why? For a website with a white background, PNG is the optimal format due to its support for transparency, lossless compression preserving image quality, and adaptability across browsers, ensuring a seamless integration with the white background while maintaining crisp details.
